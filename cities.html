The cities I have ever lived in

During my 23 years life, I have been living in 4 cities: Ann Arbor, Shanghai, Zhengzhou and Luoyang.

I was born in Luoyang in 1995. Raised in love, I spend my childhood with a lot of happy memories. When I got to 15, I moved to Zhengzhou to pursue better high school education.

Then I was admitted to Fudan University in Shanghai. Shanghai is the best cities I have lived in. I made a lot of friends there. Now I am living in ann arbor and studying in University of Michigan.
