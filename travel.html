The cities I have ever traveled to

During my whole life, I have been traveled to a lot of countries and cities and have a lot of fantastic memories.

Before I was 23, I lived in China for 22 years. And all the contries that I have visited to are in Asia:China, Japan, Thailand, Cambodian and Vietnam.
